Your precinct/jurisdiction determines which local contests and ballot measures appear on your ballot. If you moved, you may need to update your registration to vote the correct ballot. Deadlines and options differ by state, so check your official election office links. If you are at the wrong location, ask where you should vote for your current address. If you are told you are not eligible, ask what your state’s process is for provisional voting.
